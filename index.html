CyberBullying

Joe (13) and Joana (10) discover their parents, Hero and Miko, are secret CyberSafe Guards protecting children from digital threats. They face the alien villain CyberBully who preys on kids' fears online. Reporting the dangers to their parents, they get vital safety advice. Hero and Miko defeat CyberBully, revealing their secret to Joe and Joana. Inspired, the siblings vow to help others stand against cyberbullying with courage and knowledge.
